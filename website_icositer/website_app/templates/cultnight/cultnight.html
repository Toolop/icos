Cultural Night

Cultural Night is a program to introduce Indonesian culture, especially Sumatra, which is intended to introduce Indonesian culture to international audiences by adjusting the theme of ICoSITeR 2021. The sixth edition of ICoSITeR Cultural Night will be held in October 2021. This event will feature dances and other arts with cultural aspects in Indonesia. This performance was presented by ITERA students who are actively involved in their respective cultural student activity units. This Cultural Night was held to introduce Indonesian culture in the international arena. Please wait for further news on ICoSITeR's social media.
