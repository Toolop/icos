Competition



ICoSITeR (International Conference on Science, Infrastructure Technology and Regional Development) is an annual international conference held by the Sumatran Institute of Technology (ITERA) in South Lampung Regency, Lampung Province. In addition to international conferences, this event is expected to accommodate the community, especially students as the younger generation, to practise their innovation and creativity. Therefore, this series of events was created as a means to realize innovation and creativity through competitions held at national and international levels. The competitions in ICOSITER 2021 are held on an international scale which are divided into 3 fields of competitions. By joining this competition, you can make it as a form of testing the knowledge you have learned, you can also get a new relationship with international friends, improve your cooperation and collaboration skills, self-proof, self-appreciation, evaluate your weaknesses, and improve your skills. And of course you'll have the opportunity to get interesting and great prizes.

ICoSITeR Scientific Poster Design 2021 is an international competition that presents creative and innovative ideas in the form of graphic designs related to the theme of The Role of Smart City in Realizing Sustainable Development which is intended for students around the world. This activity is part of a series of ICoSITeR (International Conference on Science, Infrastructure Technology and Regional Development) events held by the Sumatra Institute of Technology (ITERA) in South Lampung, Lampung Province.

Written Idea

ICoSITeR Sumatra Institute of Technology (ITERA) also held a competition, namely Written Idea. A written idea is a concept of change or development that is futuristic, realistic, and long-term written work designed to be published in a journal or book collection of articles, written in a scientific manner adapted to applicable scientific conventions. By considering the theme "Building a Sustainable Country with the Role of Science and Technology in the Smart City Concept" as well as 5 continuous sub-themes with that theme.
