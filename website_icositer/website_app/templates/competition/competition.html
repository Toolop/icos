ICoSITeR (International Conference on Science, Infrastructure Technology and Regional Development) is an annual international conference held by the Sumatran Institute of Technology (ITERA) in South Lampung Regency, Lampung Province. In addition to international conferences, this event is expected to accommodate the community, especially students as the younger generation, to practise their innovation and creativity. Therefore, this series of events was created as a means to realize innovation and creativity through competitions held at national and international levels. The competitions in ICOSITER 2021 are held on an international scale which are divided into 2 fields of competitions. By joining this competition, you can make it as a form of testing the knowledge you have learned, you can also get a new relationship with international friends, improve your cooperation and collaboration skills, self-proof, self-appreciation, evaluate your weaknesses, and improve your skills. And of course you'll have the opportunity to get interesting and great prizes.
